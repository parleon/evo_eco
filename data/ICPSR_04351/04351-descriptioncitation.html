Uniform Crime Reports [United States]: Supplementary Homicide Reports, 1976-2003 (ICPSR 4351)

Principal Investigator(s): James Alan Fox, Northeastern University. College of Criminal Justice

Project Description:

Summary: These data provide incident-level information on criminal homicides including location, circumstances, and method of offense, as well as demographic characteristics of victims and perpetrators and the relationship between the two. The data were provided monthly to the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) by local law enforcement agencies participating in the FBI's Uniform Crime Reporting Program.
